About Us




The Department of Management Studies is organizing its annual Management Fest “MAHAPRABANDHAN PARVA” on November 15 and 16, 2019. The two days fest will include Case Study, Product Design: Idea to Execution, Guest Lectures, Role Play, Model United Nations, Model Stock Market, Business Quiz as well as Cultural Events like Group Dance, Ramp Walk and DJ Night.

About Department of Management Studies

The Department of Management Studies (DMS) was started in the year 1996 as a Centre of Management Studies and Industrial Collaboration under self-finance scheme. Since then, DMS has been playing a seminal role in the growth of corporate sector and management education in India. We groom future business leaders by following a judicious blend of theory and practice, using highly innovative teaching pedagogy. The Department offers flagship MBA and full time and part time Doctoral programmes.
